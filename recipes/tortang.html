Tortang Talong

Description

Tortang Talong is one of the most savory breakfast foods out there. In 2022, it was listed by TasteAtlas as the most popular egg dish in the world! Now you too can enjoy this juicy saut√©ed omelette any time you want!

Ingredients

  • 1 Filipino Eggplant (a Chinese or Italian eggplant will suffice)
  • 1 large egg
  • A pinch of salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 Teaspoon of oil
  • 1 clove of minced garlic

Recipe Steps

  1. Score the outside skin of the eggplant so steam can escape while broiling
  2. Place eggplant on a baking sheet and broil the eggplant in an oven for 15 minutes (turn it often!)
  3. Remove the eggplant and place it in a ziploc bag for 5 minutes (it will steam). Set aside
  4. Whisk together the egg, salt, and pepper
  5. Squish the eggplant using a fork
  6. Dip both sides of the eggplant in the whisked egg
  7. Add oil to a fying pan and heat over medium heat
  8. Add garlic. Cook for 1 minute
  9. Add the eggplant and cook until each side is browned and the egg is cooked through
  10. Serve and enjoy!
