Scrambled Eggs with Smoked Salmon

Description

Scrambled Eggs. Smoked Salmon. What more do you need? Why, some heavy cream and fresh chives. Royalty of the Brunch, this dish will leave you feeling fluffy, salty, and hungry for more.

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • 1/4 pound sliced smoked salmon
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 12 to 15 blades of fresh chives, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Chop salmon into fine pieces
  2. Whisk eggs and cream. Add half of chives to mixture, as well as salt and pepper
  3. Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat
  4. Add eggs and scramble with a wooden spoon
  5. When eggs are still mostly wet, throw in chopped salmon
  6. Cook until eggs are preferred consistency.
  7. Remove from heat. Add remaining chives for garnish.
  8. Serve while still hot!
